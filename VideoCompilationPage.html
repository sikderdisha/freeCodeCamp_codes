Introduction of JS

JavaScript, often abbreviated as JS, is a high-level, interpreted (or just-in-time compiled) programming language with first-class functions. It is a core technology of the World Wide Web, alongside HTML and CSS, and is predominantly used to enable interactive and dynamic content on websites.
